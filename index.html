Who Am I ?

About Me

I am a barefoot trimmer with 10 years of experience trimming horses' and ponies' feet of different kinds and pathologies. Contrary to the traditional farrier methods, I focus on building and growing a healthier foot rather than cutting it to standards or to look pretty. The comfort of the horse is my first priority.

What I Do ?

Service
Basic hoof trim

Suites horses with good string feet

Trimming foundered hooves

A special approach to trimming horses and ponies feet suffering from laminitis/founder

Hoof casting

Temporarily applying hoof casts made of fiberglass to stop hoof wall flaring

Hoof wall crack resection

Opening up cracks to allow oxygen from the air to help fighting with anaerobic bacterias, to prevent dirt and manure to get packed in such cracks, creating environment for bacterias to grow happily and to make it easy to apply topical antimicrobial treatment

Fitting ScootBoots

Includes measuring and finding the best fit for each hoof on-site

HoofArmour application

Protects the sole of the foot from excess wearing

Free online advice

You are welcome to ask me any questions.
